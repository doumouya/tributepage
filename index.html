The Life of Dr. Diop

  • 1923 - Born in Thieytou, Diourbel Region, Senegal

  • 1946 - Moves to Paris for his studies.

  • 1948 - Gains his first degree (bachelor) in Phylosophy in the Faculty of Arts of the University of Paris.

  • 1950 - Gains two diplomas in Chemistry in the Faculty of sciences of the University of Paris

  • 1954 - Registers a thesis where he argued that ancient Egypt had been populated by Black People. He published many of his ideas as the book Negro Nations and Culture

  • 1956 - Re-registers a new proposed thesis for Doctor of Letters titled The areas of matriarchy and Patriarchy in Ancient Times

  • 1957 - Begins specializing in Nuclear Physics at the laboratory of Nuclear Chemistry of the College de France and the Institut Pierre et Marie Curie in Paris.

  • 1960 - Obtains his doctorate

  • 1986 - Dies at the age of 62

According to Diop's own account, his education in Paris included History, Egyptology, Physics, Linguistics, Anthropology, Economics, and Sociology.
